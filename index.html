Simba kittie

Appearance:

  • Thick fur that changes color with the seasons
  • Dark grey or brown fur in the summer
  • White or cream fur in the winter
  • small ears and nose
  • Long bushy tail with short legs

Size:

  • Weight approxiamtely 8 (3.5kg) as an adult
  • About 2.5 to 3.5 feet (80 to 110cm) long

Life Span:

  • 8 to 10 years

Family:

  • Males guard dens and get food
  • Young are called kite
  • Females take care of kite in den for 2 to 4 weeks
